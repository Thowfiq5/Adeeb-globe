Area Of Operation

Adeeb Group offers adaptable contract service solutions, always determined to improve, maintain, and preserve our client’s facilities. Our service programs are tailored to our customers’ distinctive necessities.

Adeeb Group is a full-service real estate management firm offering professional Facility management and MEP services for private and institutions throughout UAE. Our award-winning, cost-effective facilities management solutions cover everything you need for your residential, retail, or commercial property. Our management team has the proven ability to oversee daily management and operations of the office, retail, industrial and multi-family properties. We realize that each owner has specific goals and objectives for their investment.

We listen, understand, and interpret those goals and tailor them to develop a realistic, practical management plan that achieves the owner's results and enhances the value of the asset. Adeeb is one of the leading facilities management companies in the UAE and has a proven track record of providing facilities management services to a range of public and private sector organizations. We work with you to understand your business or organization and then tailor a solution to meet your precise needs. We assure quality services by providing professional and independent management of your facilities and facility services. We stay on the cutting edge of industry best practices and innovative technologies to continuously improve the quality of our services, drive cost efficiency and minimize our environmental footprint.
