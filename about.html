Who We Are

Adeeb group is a leading IMS-certified Organization

Adeeb group is a leading IMS-certified Organization with ISO 9001, 14001, 41001, and ISO 45001, ISO 41001 (Facility Management), ISO 50001 (Energy Management), OSHAD, and TQM (Total Quality Management). We were awarded SKEA 2015 (Silver Category) for Facilities Management and MEP Contracting Services. Headquartered in Abu Dhabi and established in 1994, we deliver a wide range of integrated facility management and MEP contracting services across multiple sectors in the UAE and Seychelles.

We are not just providers of Facility Management and Maintenance assistance. We bring the science and art of servicing and cleaning facilities with an expert team of engineers and technicians. We are the premier source of excellent services that offer expert and reliable solutions to property management challenges.

With 28 years of extensive knowledge, reliability, and mastery in providing facility management assistance across different levels, Adeeb Group supports a wide range of properties — from villas and residential complexes to hospitals, retail outlets, and high-rise towers. Our talented technicians and engineers provide robust operation schemes and maintenance plans tailored to each asset.

We intend to preserve the quality of life for future generations while following a path of sustainable development. Our initiatives under FM promote eco-friendly measures and demonstrate that sustainable practices can also be cost-effective. Through our Check Green services we encourage the use of chemical-free cleaning products, solutions to improve indoor air quality, responsible waste disposal and recycling, and other measures to reduce costs while protecting the environment.

Working with strategic partners and professional consultants, we provide clients with platforms to exercise energy conservation methods. A comprehensive facility audit helps us develop energy-efficient measures and alternative energy solutions. We tailor solutions that integrate seamlessly into core operations from project inception through routine management, helping clients achieve their financial and sustainability goals.
