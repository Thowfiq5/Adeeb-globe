Service Support

Service Support

The Services division of Adeeb Group caters to the operations and maintenance of Life Safety Systems, Building Automation Solutions, ELV systems, and complex information technology deployments. With over 400 trained service staff, Adeeb Group undertakes both preventive and reactive maintenance harnessing the power of our 24/7 Service Desk.

Adeeb Group is maintaining mission-critical life safety systems, complex ELV and building management solutions in some of the most sensitive and high-profile facilities throughout the UAE. Four decades of experience and market-leading expertise provide building owners and stakeholders the peace of mind they expect for the smooth running of their critical building solutions.

Overview

Our service support framework is designed to keep critical systems operating safely, efficiently and continuously. We combine technical expertise, responsive coordination and lifecycle-based maintenance support to meet the operational demands of complex facilities.

After Sales Support

At Adeeb Group, we listen to customer requirements carefully and develop a customized maintenance and support solution that best fits individual customer needs, including:

  • Preventive Maintenance
  • Reactive and Corrective Maintenance
  • Spares Management
  • System Inspection and Diagnostics
  • 24/7 Operations Support
  • Staff Augmentation
  • 24/7 Remote Monitoring
  • Fully Outsourced Arrangements

24/7 Customer Service Desk Support

Adeeb Group’s maintenance teams are reachable via a 24/7 Customer Service Desk at 800 ADEEB (23332). Service desk agents log and follow up on requests through a structured support process to ensure timely responses and adherence to service expectations.

The service desk team is backed by experienced and certified engineers who are always ready to provide an immediate response, whether it is day or night.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs)

We undertake Annual Maintenance Contracts of all types, including comprehensive, non-comprehensive and semi-comprehensive arrangements. Our teams work closely with customers to fine-tune the right AMC package based on the complexity and criticality of their systems.

Service Level Agreements

Adeeb Group undertakes Annual Maintenance Contracts with a commitment to even stringent Service Level Agreements. Dedicated service coordinators are employed to manage key performance indicators associated with SLAs and ensure support continuity.
